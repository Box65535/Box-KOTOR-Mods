Speed
-Increased Speed for 18 + 3*Force
-DC 6

Sweep
-Forced4 Damage (Bludgeoning)
-Knockdown for 3 seconds
-Damage/2 and no knockdown on save (Will/Reflex, None)
-DC 8

Ionize
-Forced4 Damage (Ion)
-Stun for 6 + 0.25*Force seconds
-Damage/2 and no stun on save (Will/Fortitude, Ion)
-DC 8

Levitate
-Whirlwind for 6 + 0.25*Force seconds
-Nothing on save (Will/Reflex, None)
-DC 10

Throw Ligthsaber
-Forced8 Damage
-Damage/3 on save (Reflex, None)
-DC 10

Push
-Forced6 Damage (Bludgeoning)
-Force Push for 0.5 + 0.1*Force
-Damage/2 and no push on save (Will/Reflex, None)
-DC 12

Blind
-Defense -4 and 25% Miss Chance for 9 + 0.25*Force
-Defense -2 for 6 seconds on save (Will, None)
-DC 12

Agility
-One Extra Action per Round for 18 + 3*Force seconds
-Speed Increase Bonus if acquired
-DC 14

Battle Meditation
-Attack/Defense +3 to all Party for 18 + 3*Force seconds
-DC 14

Confusion
-Faction Swap for 12 + 0.5*Force seconds
-Nothing on save (Will, Mind-Affecting)
-Only one confusion at a time
-DC 16



Valor
-Attack/Defense +3 for 9 + Force seconds
-DC 6

Sleep
-Sleep for 3 + 0.25*Force seconds
-Attack -2 for 3 seconds on save (Will, Mind-Affecting)
-DC 8

Body
-Poison Removal/Immunity and 2 heal per 3 second
-Lasts for 18 + 3*Force
-DC 10

Suppress
-Forced6 Force Damage
-Wis/Chr -6 for 18 + 2*Force seconds
-Damage/3 and Chr -4 for 6 seconds on save (Will, Light Side)
-DC 12

Paralysis
-Paralysis for 9 + 0.5*Force seconds
-Slow for 9 seconds on save (Will, Mind-Affecting)
-DC 14

Light
-Enemy Morality is boosted +UserForce
-For each point:
	Calculate Evil = (100-Morality)/5
	Deal 1dEvil Damage (Light Side)
	If Damage would be fatal, accumulate all remaining Evil points
-Sleep for 0.1*EvilRemaining seconds
-ForcedEvil/3 Damage on save (Will, Light Side)
-DC 16

Sever
-Cutoff from force Jedi
-Impossible to save (except bosses, Will, Light Side)
-DC 18



Fury
-Attack/Damage +4 for 6 + Force seconds
-DC 6

Afflict
-Slow and -4 Str/Dex for 18 + 2*Force
-Half slow for 9 seconds on save (Fortitude/Will, Poison)
-DC 8

Fear
-Fear for 6 + 0.25*Force
-Attack -2 for 6 seconds on Save (Will, Fear)
-DC 10

Choke
-Forced6 Damage (Dark Side)
-Choke for 9 seconds
-No effect and 1/2 damage on save (Fortitude/Will, Dark Side)
-DC 12

Drain
-Forced6 Damage (Dark Side) and heal half
-Damage/2 on save (Fortitude/Will, Dark Side)
-DC 14

Lightning
-Forced12 Damage (Dark Side)
-Stun for 3 seconds
-No effect and 1/3 damage on save (Fortitude/Will, Dark Side)
-DC 16

Consume
-Kill Jedi and +1 Force Point
-3 second Fear on save save (Will, Dark Side)
-DC 18
